## Why this batch

`monitor-detect-triage` already covered low-risk watchlisting, moderate anomaly packet assembly, high-risk alert triage, and critical corroboration, but it still lacked a clean pattern for cases where a triaged packet is ready to move and the remaining work is governed release into a restricted downstream queue or command lane. `approval-gated-triage-dispatch` fits the family because it stops at the dispatch boundary, keeping response authority choice, planning, investigation, collaboration, and execution outside the workflow.
