## Why this batch

`human-agent-collaborative-work` already covered low-risk shared-workbench upkeep, high-risk approval-centered collaboration, and critical protected-room artifact collaboration, but it still lacked a clean pattern for cases where the collaborative artifact itself becomes release-ready and the remaining work is governed human approval to send that exact revision into one bounded downstream lane. `approval-gated-collaborative-artifact-release` fits the family because it keeps shared co-production, disagreement preservation, and release ownership central while leaving recommendation adjudication, transformation, and live action to adjacent patterns.
